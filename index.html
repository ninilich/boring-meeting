Why BoringMeeting?

Tired of endless Town Halls and all-hands meetings dragging on forever? BoringMeeting captures Teams/Zoom/etc. or any system audio locally on your Mac. Get clean, accurate transcripts—no fluff, no cloud nonsense.

🔒 Local Everything

Record from MS Teams, Google Meet, Zoom, web browser, any app. AI transcription runs offline—nothing leaves your Mac.

⚡ One-Tap Simplicity

Start/stop with one click. Get plain text transcripts. No accounts, no cloud overhead.

Key Features

  • Capture audio from Teams, Meet, Zoom, browsers, any app
  • Offline transcription: English, German, Russian, Spanish, French + more
  • Background recording—switch apps freely
  • Requires Apple Silicon (M1+)
  • 100% privacy—no data leaves your Mac

Quick Start

  1. Open BoringMeeting
  2. First run: download offline model + grant mic permissions
  3. Click "Start New Recording"
  4. Stop → "Start Transcription" → get text in minutes
  5. Paste into ChatGPT, notes, or company AI
