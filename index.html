How it works

Four steps to freedom

Seriously, that's it.

1

Open BoringMeeting

On first run: download the offline transcription model and grant permissions. Takes a minute.

2

Hit "Start New Recording"

Join your meeting. Switch apps freely. BoringMeeting records in the background.

3

Stop → Start Transcription

Offline AI processes the audio on your Mac. Takes a few minutes depending on recording length and the model you chose.

4

Use your text

Paste into ChatGPT, your company AI, Notion, or just read it yourself. Zero fluff, pure content.
