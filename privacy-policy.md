# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 01, 2026**

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy for BoringMeeting describes how we handle user information. We **do not collect**, **store**, or **share** any personal data.

## Data Collection
The app operates primarily locally on the user's device.
- No personal data (such as name, email, contacts, photos, or location) is collected.
- We do not create user accounts or use any in-app tracking or analytics.

## Internet Connection and Downloads
The app requires an internet connection only for setup to download open source AI/ML models that are needed for the app to function.
- These downloads are made directly from external sources that provide the open source models.
- We do not collect or link any personal data to these downloads.
- We do not use these network requests for tracking, profiling, or advertising.

## Data Usage
Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data usage to describe.
Any data processed by the app (for example, inputs you provide to the models) is handled locally on your device and is not sent to our own servers.

## Third Parties
We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or SDKs that collect personal data.
Open source model providers may have their own terms and licenses for the models themselves, but they do not receive your personal data from us.

## Policy Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Please check this page for the latest version.
